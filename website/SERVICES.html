Service Styles

Table Service:

A server takes orders and brings food to the table. It ranges from basic to elaborate service styles.

Counter Service:

Guests order and pick up their food at a counter (common in fast food).

Buffet/All-You-Can-Eat:

Guests serve themselves from a selection of dishes.

Takeout and Delivery:

Food is ordered for off-premise dining, a service that has become very popular.
